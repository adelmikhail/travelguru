About Us

Our Company

Travelguru is a travel recommendation platform that offers personalized trip ideas and hidden gems worldwide. Using community reviews, AI-driven itineraries, real-time local tips, collaborative trip boards, and verified deals, it simplifies planning so users spend more time experiencing and less time researching.
